System Capabilities

Advanced monitoring and control at your fingertips.

Water Level Monitoring

Real-time ultrasonic distance sensing to track exactly how much water is in your tank.

Smart Pump Control

Automated relay switching prevents overflow and dry runs. Manual override available.

Energy Tracking

PZEM004T sensor integration to monitor voltage, current, and total power consumed by the pump.

Detailed Reports

Weekly, monthly, and yearly insights on water usage and electricity consumption.

How It Works

Seamless data flow from hardware to your screen.

1

Hardware Sensors

Distance sensors and power meters collect raw data from the tank and pump.

2

Microcontroller

Processes the sensor data and executes local automation rules safely.

3

HTTP Client

Transmits the processed data securely over the network to the server.

4

SaaS Dashboard

Data is visualized beautifully with glassmorphism UI for monitoring and control.
